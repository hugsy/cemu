CEMU allows you to play easily with assembly on many architectures (x86, ARM, MIPS, etc.), compile execute the result in an emulated environment.
It is a perfect tool to learn assembly, load raw binary dumps captured, or build your custom shellcodes.

The software is distributed freely under the MIT license. This tool relies on the fantastic libraries/frameworks:
  • Keystone
  • Capstone
  • Unicorn
  • Pygments
  • PyQt6
